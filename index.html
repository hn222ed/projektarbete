Terese Elina Jansson is a Swedish illustrator based in Stockholm, drawing fashion, accessories, hair, make-up, objects/interiors and more. Besides being an illustrator, Terese studies architecture at KTH Royal Institute of Technology. For further inquiries regarding orders, prices, projects and upcoming work please go to contact.

Some of the illustrations have appeared in magazines and movies. Art displayed on this website is a sample of a bigger portfolio.

Watch the slideshow of project work by Terese as an architecture. CV and work proposals can be sent upon request.
